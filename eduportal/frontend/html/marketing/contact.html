Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Is EduPortal really free?

Yes — completely free for every learner, parent, teacher, and school in South Sudan. There is no subscription, no paywall, and no hidden fees. This will never change.

How do I get my school listed?

Contact us using the form above or email hello@eduportal.ss. Our admin team will verify your school and send an invitation to set up your school admin account.

How can my NGO post scholarships?

Visit the Partner With Us page or contact us directly. We'll verify your organisation and set up an NGO officer account so you can post and manage scholarships.

Does the platform work on slow internet connections?

Yes. EduPortal is designed to work on 2G connections. Pages are lightweight, images are compressed, and study materials can be downloaded for offline use.
