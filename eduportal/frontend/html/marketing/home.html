What EduPortal does

Everything a South Sudan learner needs in one place

Whether you are a student in Juba or a parent in a rural county, EduPortal gives you the same access to the same verified information.

Searchable School Directory

Filter every registered school by state, county, level, type, and boarding option. View full profiles, admission requirements, and contact details.

Study Materials Repository

Past exam papers, study guides, and teacher notes organised by subject, grade, and year. Download or preview — no subscription required.

Scholarship Finder

Search and apply for scholarships from verified NGOs. Filter by eligibility, deadline, and state. Track every application in one dashboard.

Announcement Board

Live notices from the Ministry of Education, SSNEC, and registered schools. Filter by audience and date. Never miss a registration deadline.

Admission Checklists

Each school profile includes a printable admission checklist showing exactly which documents to bring — downloadable as a PDF.

Role-Based Access

Six tailored experiences: student, parent, teacher, school admin, NGO officer, and platform admin. Each role sees exactly what they need.

Our mission

Bridging the information gap in South Sudan's education

South Sudan has an adult literacy rate of approximately 34%. Millions of learners cannot access school directories, past exam papers, or scholarship information — not because it doesn't exist, but because no one has put it in one place.

EduPortal South Sudan was built to fix that. One verified, free, low-bandwidth platform for every learner in every state.
