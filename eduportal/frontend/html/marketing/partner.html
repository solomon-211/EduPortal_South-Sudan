Who we partner with

Four ways to partner

Every partnership is free. We believe in collaboration, not commercialisation.

Schools

School Partnership

List your school on EduPortal and give every parent and student in South Sudan access to your profile, admission requirements, and announcements.

  • Free verified school profile
  • Manage admission requirements
  • Post announcements to students & parents
  • Dedicated school admin dashboard
NGOs

NGO Partnership

Post scholarship listings and reach every eligible learner in the country simultaneously. No advertising budget required.

  • Post unlimited scholarship listings
  • Receive applications directly on the platform
  • Verified NGO badge on all listings
  • Dedicated NGO officer dashboard
Government

Government Partnership

Ministry of Education and SSNEC can post official announcements, exam schedules, and policy updates directly to all registered users.

  • Official Ministry announcement channel
  • Reach students, teachers, and parents
  • Verified government badge
  • Priority placement on announcement board
Donors

Donor & Funder Partnership

Support EduPortal's mission to keep the platform free for every learner in South Sudan. Your funding directly sustains the infrastructure.

  • Named acknowledgement on the platform
  • Impact reporting and analytics
  • Co-branding opportunities
  • Direct line to the founding team
